Introduction

The first purpose of the decomposition is develop an understand of what is driving changes in headline and core inflation. The second purpose is to have a handy reference.

Core Inflation excludes energy and food. Food is defined as all food and non-beverages, i.e., it excludes alchoholic beverages.
